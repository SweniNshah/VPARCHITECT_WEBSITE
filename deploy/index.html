The Studio — 02

Two architects, one quiet
obsession.

Founded in 2018 by Piyush & Vaishali Shah, V P Architects is a small Mumbai studio by choice — deeply involved in every drawing that leaves the desk.

We believe good architecture is felt before it is seen — in the way morning light falls across a wall, in the hush of a well-proportioned room, in materials left honest enough to age gracefully.

Piyush Shah

Principal Architect · Co-founder

Leads design & site craft. Trained at Rizvi College of Architecture, Mumbai, Piyush is drawn to raw material, structural clarity and the discipline of the section.

Vaishali Shah

Principal Architect · Co-founder

A partner in the practice, Vaishali leads project planning and delivery — bringing warmth, rigour and a sharp eye for detail from first sketch to finished building.
