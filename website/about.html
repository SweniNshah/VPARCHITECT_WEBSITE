About Us

A studio built on partnership.

Founded and led by two architects — partners in life and in practice — for twenty-five years.

Our Story

Twenty-five years, one shared vision.

We began the studio in 2000 with a simple conviction: that good architecture is quiet, contextual and generous. Over twenty-five years we have grown into a close team while keeping the intimacy of a partner-led practice — every project passes through both our hands.

Our work spans private homes, workplaces, hospitality and public spaces, but the approach is constant: begin with the place, respect the climate, choose honest materials, and design for the people who will live and work within.
